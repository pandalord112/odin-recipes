Smash Burgers

In order to make good smash burgers we first need 80/20 ground beef. The fat will help keep evertyhing nice and juicy. Then we need a cheese that melts well, preferably American cheese. Pickles are required and I prefer to use sweet pickle chips. Lastly we need onions that we are going to carmalize. Optionally we can add a burger sauce. I prefer to make my own. It will include ketchup, mayonaise, mustard, pickle juice, onion powder, garlic powder, and pepper.
